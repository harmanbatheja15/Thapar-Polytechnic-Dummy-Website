Description

We have a full fledged Training & Placement cell which plays an important role in counseling the students regarding their career and placements. On an average 70 - 80% of our eligible students got placements in the past three years.
We have the entire infrastructure required for campus recruitments and other T & P activities like : Conference Hall, Overhead Projectors, PA system, Online Test Facility, Video Conferencing etc. We have assigned one staff member from each department/trade as representative of TP cell to assist the students and recruiters during campus p lacements and other activities organized by training and placement cell.
