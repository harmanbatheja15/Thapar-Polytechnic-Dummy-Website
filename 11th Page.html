  1. Applied Science

Department of applied sciences provides knowledge in the areas of physics, chemistry, mathematics and communication skills. The faculty of applied sciences department is highly qualified, experienced, motivated and dedicated. The faculty of department is actively working to provide quality education and supporting co-curricular activities and extracurricular activities. Department has well equipped Physics Lab, Chemistry Lab and Communication Skill Lab to cater the needs of the students of 1st & 2nd semesters of all the disciplines being offered at TPC. Students are given practical training in laboratories.
