  1. Civil Engineering

Department aims at providing Quality Technical Education for producing professionally sound and morally strong diploma engineers in Civil Engineering having knowledge and skills agreeable to real world. Department has well equipped labs of Applied Mechanics, Building Construction, Construction Material, Surveying, Concrete Technology and has fully developed Project Lab also. The Department has been making use of Highway Engineering, Fluid Mechanics and Structure labs of Thapar university for conducting practicals. Faculty of Civil Engineering Department has been making continuous efforts to improve quality learning.
