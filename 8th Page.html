Boys Hostel

Boys hostel has 40 rooms. It is in the campus only, with lush green lawns.Hostel fee is Rs. 25450 p.a (room rent) + 5000 (Security Refundable). 24 hours security is provided by providing Guard Room at the entrance of the hostel and guard’s are there on rotation duty.

Capacity:-

Boys hostel has a capacity of 120 students. The accommodation is provided to the students in three-seated rooms. So total rooms are 40 in No. for the students.

Facilities:-
  1. Color T.V, Newspaper.
  2. Table Tennis Room
  3. R.O. (Water purifier), water cooler.
  4. Geyser’s fitted in wash rooms for hot water in winter.
  5. Play ground
  6. 24 hrs. Water & Electricity supply is there.


Girls Hostel

Girls hostel has 10 rooms. It is in the campus only, with lush green lawns & is a single story building. Hostel fee is Rs. 25450 p.a (room rent) + 5000 (Security Refundable). 24 hours security is provided by providing Guard Room at the entrance of the hostel and guard’s are there on rotation duty.

Capacity:-

Girls hostel has a capacity of 30 students. The accommodation is provided to the students in three-seated rooms. So total rooms are 10 in No. for the students.

Facilities:-
  1. Color T.V, Newspaper.
  2. Fully automatic washing machine.
  3. 165 lt. capacity Fridge.
  4. R.O. (Water purifier), water cooler.
  5. Geyser’s fitted in wash rooms for hot water in winter.
  6. Facility of power backup ( Generator) in case Electricity goes off.
  7. 24 hrs. Water & Electricity supply is there.
