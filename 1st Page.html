Welcome to Thapar Polytechnic College

Thapar Polytechnic College came into existence in 1956 due to the missionary zeal of the visionary Late Lala Karam Chand Thapar. It is one of the three organizations on Thapar Technology Campus, the other two are Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology University and Thapar Centre for Industrial Research & Development. It has been registered as a Society under Registration of Societies Act 1860. TPC is a privately managed, Government aided institution affiliated to Punjab State Board of Technical Education and Industrial Training, Chandigarh. During the past over five decades, TPC has established itself into a leading Polytechnic offering 3-year Diploma in 05 disciplines i.e. Civil Engg., Electrical Engg. Mechanical Engg, Computer Engg, Architectural Assistantship with a total intake of 360 students. Thapar Polytechnic College adjudged the Best Polytechnic in Northern Region in the year 1999-2000 and 2005-06.
